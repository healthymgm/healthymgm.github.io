A HEALTHY LIFE MEANS A HEALTHY WALLET

ABOUT

Our website is made to inform people facing hardships in their financial life of ways that they can make their healthcare far more affordable. Whether its through making healthier choices in their diets, exercising more, or cutting unhealthy habits out of their lives, all we want to do is help.

WHY

Health Care costs in America are rising more and more every year. Americans pay the most for health care out of everybody in the entire world. The thing about many of this disease that Americans are getting can be easily averted with the right diet and exercise.

HOW TO

For a person to be healthy they need to eat right. This means a lot of green veggies and lean meat and try to avoid calorie packed food and junk food as much as possible. Exercise is also a very vital role in a healthy life style. Now we know that many people are busy with their lives and have little time to go to the gym. So that is why we came up with a way to get some exercise when watching TV. You do a different sort of exercise each time a certain commercial comes on. For example if it is a car commercial you do jumping jacks, a commercial for a clothing store push ups so on and so forth.
